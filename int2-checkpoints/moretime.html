If I had more time?

I had a numerous amount of ideas when it came to making the art piece, but the limited time we were given only allowed me to implement some of them. One thing I would add if I had more time was to make the rosary longer. I wanted to make the rosary more detailed and longer, which would make the art more visually appealing to viewers. Another thing I would have done was added more detail to the main star in the flag. If you havem't noticed, the star on the flag is a little different compared to the real Philippine flag. I would have worked on the star more, however the limited experience I had with Javascript at the time and the limited work time I had left for the project inhibited my abiltiy to fufill that choice.
