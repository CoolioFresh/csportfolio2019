What I created


I created a Philippine flag that alternates colors when you click on the screen. Also, when you click, a part of a rosary spawns. The reason
why I chose to make a Filipino flag is because I am filipino, and I wanted to represent my nationality. I made the whole thing using Javascript and
RGB colors. In this website, I'll be showing you how I made this art piece, explain the code, my experience with Javascript, and what I would've done
if I had more time.
