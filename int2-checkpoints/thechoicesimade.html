The choices I made

The main choices I made throughout the projects were mainly motivated and influenced by my desire to express myself through art and to make it visually appealing. One choice I made was to make the Filipino flag as the main center piece of my art. I chose to make this because I feel like it represents me the most because me and my parents are Filipino, and I wouldn't be here if it weren't for their hard work and dedication. Another choice I made was to add a rosary to the art. I was motivated to make this because I am Catholic, and God is a huge part in my life. Being Catholic is a big part of me and it influences my decisions everyday, so it made sense to add something related to it. One last choice I made was to make the flag change color. I wanted this to happen because I felt that it would make the project fun and visually appealing by making it change color and interactive, and it did in the end after I added everything. All these choices I made were to express myself and make the art visually appealing and fun to play with, which will contribute to me getting a good grade on it.
