About Me

I, Nathan David, was born on April 5th, 2003 in Redwood City. For the first 3-4 years of my life, it was pretty boring for
me. I really didn't have anything I liked or did, until my dad bought a computer. He started playing the original Starcraft
on it, and my 4 year old mind was instantly captivated by that video game. I was also introduced to Anime,
which explains the wierd cat thing on the bottom of the page. Ever since then I've been hooked on video games
of any kind. MMO's, FPS', MOBA's, you name it, i've played it. At first, I was only into console games because I couldn't afford
an expensive gaming PC. But as I got older, I managed to buy a PC with saved up allowance. I then discovered programming, coding,
and how experts on it can make over six figures a year easily from just a computer. I decided to enroll in a programming class in my
high school to learn how to code. In just a week, I learned the basics of HTML and CSS, which is running this website. If it weren't
for this class, you wouldn't be reading this right now.
